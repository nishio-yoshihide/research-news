## 事実要約

The paper frames software-agent capability as a property of the model-harness-environment system and defines eleven harness responsibilities, including context selection, permissions, observability, verification, failure attribution, and intervention recording. It also proposes a four-level harness ladder and trace-based evaluation packages.
