## 事実要約

AgentCanary evaluates autonomous agents in executable environments using an Entry-by-Impact risk taxonomy and trajectory-level scoring for outcome safety, security awareness, and task utility. Its experiments report persistent weaknesses against compromised skills, persistent-state attacks, and long-horizon attacks.
